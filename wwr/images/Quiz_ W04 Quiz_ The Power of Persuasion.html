Started: Oct 4 at 8:14pm

Quiz Instructions

  • Task: Watch a video and take a quiz on rhetorical terms.
  • Purpose: Facilitate learning for rhetorical analysis assignments.

Instructions

  1. Watch "Mr. Rogers and the Power of Persuasion" Links to an external site. by Will Schoder. (23:40 mins, "Mr. Rogers and the Power of Persuasion" Transcript Links to an external site.)
  2. Take notes; when you write the rhetorical analysis, you will need to notice many of the same rhetorical appeals Schoder highlights as he analyzes Mr. Rogers' speech.
  3. Complete the quiz by the due date.
    • This quiz is open-note and you can refer back to the video (or video transcript) as you watch it.
    • You may take this quiz as many times as necessary. The scores will be averaged.
